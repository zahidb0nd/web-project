About The Project

ClubConnect is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to streamline club and event management within a college campus. It addresses the challenge of disconnected communication between students and clubs by providing a single, unified system for discovery, engagement, and administration. Our goal is to foster a more vibrant and organized campus community.

A Platform for Everyone

Students

Discover clubs, join events, and stay connected with campus life, all in one place.

Coordinators

Manage members, create events, and communicate with your club effortlessly.

Admins

Oversee all club activities and gain insights into campus-wide engagement.

System Workflow

1. Discover & Join

Students browse a directory of all campus clubs, view their profiles, and join with a single click.

2. Engage & Participate

Club coordinators create and publish events. Students can view event calendars and register for workshops, seminars, and competitions.

3. Manage & Communicate

Coordinators manage their member lists, post announcements, and track event registrations through a dedicated dashboard.

4. Oversee & Analyze

College administrators have a global view of all club activities, member statistics, and engagement trends across the campus.
